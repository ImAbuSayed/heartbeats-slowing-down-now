[Hook]
I watch the embers fading in the dark
I feel the fire waking in my heart
The heavy breathing tears us both apart
My heartbeats slowing down now

[Verse 1]
We left the noise a thousand miles away
Nothing but timber and the words we say
I see the moon reflection in your eyes
Taking my breath beneath the open skies

[Pre-Chorus]
Every single minute brings me closer to the edge
Hanging onto every little whisper that you said
I can feel the gravity between us taking hold
Drawing out the passion in the stories we unfold

[Chorus]
My heartbeats slowing down now
When you pull me in and wrap your arms around now
We don't need a map to figure where we're bound now
I just want to lose myself in what we found now
